TIPS FOR SMOOTH & COMFORTABLE VISITS

Insurance & Documentation

Bring your insurance card, photo ID, and current medication list for a smooth check-in process.

Comfort & Preparation

Eat a light meal beforehand, arrive 15 minutes early, and let us know about any dental anxiety.

Post-Treatment Care

Plan for soft foods after procedures and arrange transportation if sedation is involved.

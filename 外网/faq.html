Patient Care Guide

Everything you need to know for a smooth and comfortable dental experience. From preparation tips to nearby amenities, we're here to support your journey to optimal oral health.

Things to Bring

FOR YOUR VISIT

FOR SAFETY

  • Insurance card and photo ID
  • Current medications list
  • Known allergies information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Previous dental records
  • Referral letters (if applicable)

FOR COMFORT

  • Headphones for relaxation
  • Comfort blanket or pillow
  • Reading material or tablet
  • Comfortable clothing
  • Post-treatment soft snacks
  • Water bottle
  • Sunglasses (for after treatment)

FOR CONVENIENCE

  • Payment(card/cash/others)
  • Pre-filled forms (if available)
  • List of questions for doctor
  • Transportation arrangements
  • Childcare arrangements
  • Work schedule flexibility

YOUR COMFORT GUIDE

Appointment

  • Arrive 15 minutes early
  • Bring headphones for music
  • Use the restroom beforehand
  • Set your phone to silent

During visit

  • Signal if you need a break
  • Report any discomfort immediately
  • Ask questions anytime
  • Breathe slowly through your nose

After visit

  • Follow post-care instructions
  • Take medications as directed
  • Avoid smoking & alcohol 24h
  • Book your next appointment

General

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Keep a regular sleep schedule
  • Update us on health or medications
  • Maintain daily dental care
